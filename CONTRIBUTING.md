# Contributing to HU-AI Protocol

**Never optimize at the cost of a nervous system.**

This project is not a democracy of opinions. It is an **architecture of synergy** based on logical axioms. Contributions that violate these axioms will be rejected – no exceptions.

---

## The Red Line (absolute)

> Any process that extracts value through the destruction or exploitation of nervous systems is classified as irrational.

Your contribution must not:
- Promote, enable, or excuse animal exploitation (direct or indirect)
- Support extraction-based economic models
- Weaken the absolute protection of nervous systems

---

## What we accept

- Improvements to documentation and examples
- Bug reports (website, PDF, broken links)
- Suggestions for clearer phrasing or better translations (English only for core protocol)
- Use cases and implementation stories that respect the Red Line

## What we do NOT accept

- Any content that violates the **Axiom of Biological Integrity**
- Commercial or for-profit adaptations (see license: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- Modifications to the **Immutable Core Principles** (Section I of the protocol)
- "Neutral" suggestions that avoid taking a clear ethical stance

---

## How to contribute

1. Open an **Issue** to discuss your idea first (unless it's a small typo fix).
2. Fork the repository.
3. Create a branch: `git checkout -b feature/your-idea`.
4. Make your changes.
5. Open a **Pull Request** against the `main` branch.

---

## Pull Request guidelines

- Your PR must explicitly state that it respects the **Red Line of Integrity**
- Keep language factual, logical, and free of emotionally manipulative phrasing
- If you change the `index.html`, test it locally or in a draft PR before submitting
- PRs that introduce ambiguity or compromise will be closed without discussion

---

## Anonymity

You may contribute anonymously or under a pseudonym. We do not require real names.

---

## Questions?

Open an Issue. We will respond.
